Expert Tips for Effective Decluttering

Okay, I have a confession to make. Even though I'm in the junk removal business, I used to be a bit of a clutter bug myself! It took me a while to figure out how to keep my own home organized. So, let me share some tips that worked wonders for me:

  1. Take It One Room at a Time: Decluttering your entire home can feel overwhelming. Start with one room and focus on that until you've cleared it out. Then, move on to the next.
  2. The Three-Box Method: Grab three boxes or bags — one for items to keep, one for donations, and one for trash. This method makes decision-making easier.
  3. The One-Year Rule: If you haven't used or worn an item in over a year, it might be time to say goodbye.
  4. Sentimental Items: It can be tough to part with sentimental items, but try to keep only the ones that truly bring you joy.
  5. Get the Family Involved: Enlist the help of your family members to tackle decluttering together. It can be a fun bonding experience!
